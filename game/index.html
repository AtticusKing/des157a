GAME OF PIG

Score

0 - 0

Directions

First to 30! Try your luck rolling the dice or pass for free pts.

The total of each role is added to your score unless either die comes up as “one”. If this happens, your turn is over and you don’t get any more points. Roll two “ones” and your entire score will be erased! So play carefully, and if you think your luck is running thin, pass to get 3 pts without the risk of losing.
