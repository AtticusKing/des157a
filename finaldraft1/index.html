Cosmere

A great region in space whose boundaries are set by the four stars with livable exoplanets - the known expanse of mankind throughout the stars.

Dantu

The untamed world, the Emberlan Expanse is a vast region of tundra and forests seen by few. One of the few places in Cosmere where flora and fauna is more abundant than people.

Eurus

A world of water, the single large continent is host to the vast 10,000 year old capital city called Auran. The few other cities are established across hundreds of small islands.

Paibu

A world of jungle wood and sweltering heat, long ago the great stone city of Caran’daal rose high above the vast canopy but today its mysteriously silent.

Castan

The homeworld of all mankind, a great ecumenopolis of concrete and neon. The once green and natural surface of the entire world now sits in the darkness of the buried “first level”.
